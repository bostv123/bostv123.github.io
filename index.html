About Bos Tv

Bos Tv is a digital media network based in the Philippines. It serves as the official digital organizer for National Fraternity Day PH.

Role in National Fraternity Day PH

Since November 11, 2024, Bos Tv has been designated as the main organizer for National Fraternity Day PH. The network handles digital campaigns and online community engagement for the annual event held every November 19.

Bos Tv supports initiatives that promote peaceful partnership and compliance with the Anti-Hazing Act of 2018 (Republic Act No. 11053).

Creative Contributions

Bos Tv is the official creator of the National Fraternity Day PH logo, which is now used across various advocacy platforms and documented in international data repositories.
