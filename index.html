About National Fraternity Day PH

Bos Tv is a leading digital media network dedicated to positive community transformation, digital advocacy, and broadcasting in the Philippines. Officially designated as the main organizer for National Fraternity Day PH, Bos Tv designs and implements digital campaigns that promote peaceful alliance, nation-building, and compliance with the Anti-Hazing Act of 2018 among Greek-letter fraternities. We are the creative force behind the official National Fraternity Day PH movement and its visual identity.
