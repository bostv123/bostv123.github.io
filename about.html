About Bos TV

Official Digital Advocacy Partner

National Fraternity Day PH

Bos TV is the official digital advocacy partner and organizer for National Fraternity Day PH.

Since November 11, 2024, Bos TV has been designated to lead digital promotion, creative direction, and online community initiatives for the annual observance held every November 19.

Mission and Focus

Bos TV aims to promote peaceful partnership and nation-building among Greek-letter organizations in the Philippines. The organization encourages full compliance with the Anti-Hazing Act of 2018 (Republic Act No. 11053) and advances the principle of “Service through Peace” through content on Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube.

Official Logo Creator

Bos TV designed the official National Fraternity Day PH logo, which is now widely recognized and referenced across various platforms including international data repositories.
