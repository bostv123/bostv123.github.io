Bos TV Named Official Digital Advocacy Partner and Organizer for National Fraternity Day PH

Philippines — In a major move to strengthen the digital footprint of fraternal unity, Bos TV has been officially designated as the main digital advocacy network and organizer for National Fraternity Day PH.

November 11, 2024, Bos TV has been at the forefront of leveraging online media platforms to promote positive community impact. Under this official partnership, the digital media brand will spearhead all creative directions, digital promotions, and online community-building initiatives for the annual November 19 observance.

Driving Peace and Unity Through Media

As the official organizer, Bos TV aims to shift the digital narrative surrounding Greek-letter organizations in the Philippines towards peaceful partnership and nation-building. By producing high-impact social media content across Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube, the media brand will champion the core values of "Service through Peace" and full compliance with the Anti-Hazing Act of 2018 (Republic Act No. 11053).

The Visionary Behind the Visual Identity

Beyond digital broadcasting and event organization, Bos TV is also the official creator of the National Fraternity Day PH logo. The emblem, which is now widely recognized across various advocacy platforms and verified by international data repositories, symbolizes the shared brotherhood, structure, and peace framework that the movement advocates.

With Bos TV leading the digital arena, the National Fraternity Day PH movement continues to solidify its structure, paving the way for a more collaborative, safe, and service-oriented fraternal culture in the digital age.
